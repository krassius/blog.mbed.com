<p>
  Last week mbed was at <a href="http://www.ubmdesign.com/" rel=
  "nofollow">ESC Design West</a> in San Jose. The conference hosted
  a number of distinguished speakers covering a range of
  interesting topics and 250+ exhibitors showcasing the latest in
  embedded technology.<br>
  <br>
  <img src=
  "https://developer.mbed.org/media/uploads/anaran/_scaled_exhibition_centre.jpg"
  alt="/media/uploads/anaran/_scaled_exhibition_centre.jpg" title=
  "/media/uploads/anaran/_scaled_exhibition_centre.jpg"><br>
  <br>
</p>
<p>
  <br>
  The ARM stand was packed with the latest tools demos for <a href=
  "http://www.arm.com/products/tools/software-tools/mdk-arm/index.php"
  rel="nofollow">Keil MDK</a> and <a href=
  "http://www.arm.com/products/tools/software-tools/ds-5/index.php"
  rel="nofollow">ARM Development Studio 5 (DS-5)</a>, as well a
  hardware demo for the latest <a href=
  "http://www.arm.com/products/processors/cortex-m/cortex-m0plus.php"
  rel="nofollow">ARM Cortex-M0+ Processor</a>
</p>
<p>
  <img src=
  "https://developer.mbed.org/media/uploads/anaran/_scaled_arm_stand.jpg"
  alt="/media/uploads/anaran/_scaled_arm_stand.jpg" title=
  "/media/uploads/anaran/_scaled_arm_stand.jpg">
</p>
<p>
  We had a lot of interest in mbed for those looking to develop
  with 32-bit ARM MCU's. Most visitors were impressed by the ease
  of prototyping <a href="http://mbed.org/cookbook/IOT">IoT
  applications</a> using HTML5 websockets over <a href=
  "/blog/cookbook/Internet-of-things-using-Ethernet">Ethernet</a>,
  <a href="/cookbook/Internet-of-things-using-Wifi">Wi-Fi</a> and
  3G Mobile Internet.
</p>
<p>
  Once again our partners NXP had a blast demoing the <a href=
  "/handbook/mbed-NXP-LPC11U24">LPC11U24</a> mbed-based <a href=
  "/cookbook/Slingshot">Slingshot</a>. The slingshot is earmarked
  for another couple engagements, so keep an eye out if you want to
  have a go!
</p>
<p>
  Also a big congratulations to NXP for winning a <a href=
  "http://ubm-ace.com/winners.php" rel="nofollow">UBM Electronics
  ACE Award</a> for the Cortex-M0 <a href=
  "http://www.nxp.com/products/microcontrollers/cortex_m0/lpc11u00/"
  rel="nofollow">LPC11U00 Microcontroller Family</a>. The award
  winners were announced during the show and NXP took out the award
  in the Digital ICs (MCUs, FPGAs, microprocessors) section.
</p>
